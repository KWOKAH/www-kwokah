Do The Right Thing

Providing a safe work environment with appropriate working hours and an honest, living wage are obviously the absolute bare minima ... sadly many fast-fashion factories already stumble at this, seemingly low hurdle.

The factories we partner with, look after their staff and actively support their local communities.

Do It Well

Sustainability's unsung hero is for everyone to actually buy less stuff. To consume less we need better quality products that last longer.

Our production partners are equipped with modern-tech and lean processes to efficiently produce top-notch quality products.

Do Things Right

The textile industry has a very poor environmental track record. Often with excessive water use, irresponsible use of chemicals and CO2 pollution.

Our factories must have proper processes and systems in place to reduce water consumption, manage waste water, avoid or handle chemicals responsibly, and use renewable energy.

Regular external audits and industry certifications are required.
